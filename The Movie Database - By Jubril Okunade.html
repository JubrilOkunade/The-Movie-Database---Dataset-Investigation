#Create a function to plot visualizations for each property

def graph(dframe,prop,y):
    for col in prop:
        plt.scatter(dframe[col],dframe[y])
        plt.title(f'Relationship between {col} and {y}')
        plt.xlabel(f'{col}')
        plt.ylabel(f'{y}')
        plt.show()

Conclusions¶

    The following conclusions can be drawn from the analysis:
  • There revenue of high revenues(hi_rev) shows a strong positive correlation with the budget and the popularity.
  • There revenue of high revenues(hi_rev) shows a weak correlation with the runtime and the vote_average.
  • There popularity of high popularity((po_hi) shows a weak correlation with the budget, runtime and vote_average.
  • there was a significant spike in the movies that made high revenues from the 1970's onwards.
  • We can see an increasing trend in the revenue from an over all perspective.
